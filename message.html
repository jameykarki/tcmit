ADVANTAGES OFBIKING

Advantage: Less Repairs:- Motor bikes need less repairs and maintenance than an automobile. Unlike a regular car, oil changes are relatively simple and can be done by the owner rather than a specialized mechanic. Repairs for motor bikes also cost less than car repairs.

Advantage: Parking:- When you are in the big city there is limited parking. Often if you have a larger car you cannot always easily find parking space. With a motor bike you can park anywhere you like and often there are special parking stalls just for motocycles.

Advantage: Traffic:- Traffic is always a problem when you need to get somewhere. With a motor bike you no longer have to sit behind a sea of cars waiting for the traffic to get moving. Motor bikes can travel in between cars parked on the highway rather than waiting for their turn to move.

Advantage: Cheaper Licensing and Taxes:- Registration and taxes for motor bikes are considerably less than for automobiles. Since motor bikes do not use as much fuel, there are government discounts on registering motor bikes.
